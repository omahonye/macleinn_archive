# AMLÉ / USI Officer History

A historical listing of Aontas na Mac Léinn in Éirinn (AMLÉ) and formerly the Union of Students in Ireland (USI) executive officers from 1959 to present.

## About

This record compiles the known executive officers of AMLÉ (formerly USI) from 1959 to present.

**Sources:** compiled from archived versions of usi.ie and amle.ie, Congress records, annual reports, student media, alumni input, and historical publications.

## Notes

- Years are academic years.

- Officer structures and titles have changed several times throughout the organisation's history.
- Home Students' Union refers to the member students' union from which an officer was elected.
- Corrections or additions are welcome.
