# Trinity College Dublin Students' Union Officer History

A historical listing of Trinity College Dublin Students' Union (Aontas Mac Léinn Choláiste na Tríonóide), often referred to by its abbreviation TCDSU/AMLCT, executive and representative officers from foundation to present.

## About

This record compiles the known executive officers of Trinity College Dublin Students' Union from foundation to present.

**Sources:** compiled from archived versions of tcdsu.org, Trinity College Dublin publications, student media, election records, alumni input, and TCDSU communications.
